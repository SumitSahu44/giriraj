Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

FinPay uses bank-grade security protocols with 256-bit encryption for all transactions. We're PCI-DSS compliant and implement multiple layers of security including biometric authentication, OTP verification, and real-time fraud detection systems to ensure your money and data remain completely secure.

FinPay accepts a wide range of payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), UPI, net banking, digital wallets, and even cash on delivery for certain services. You can also link your bank accounts directly for seamless transactions.

Our customer support is available 24/7 through multiple channels. You can reach us via in-app chat support, email at support@finpay.com, or call our toll-free number 1-800-FINPAY. For urgent issues, our live chat typically responds within 2 minutes.

Most transactions on FinPay are completely free, including UPI payments, mobile recharges, and utility bill payments. For certain premium services like instant money transfers to non-FinPay users or international transactions, a small convenience fee may apply. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm any transaction.

You earn FinPay points on every transaction, with bonus points for referring friends, completing challenges, and shopping from partner merchants. Points can be redeemed for cashback, discount vouchers, or exclusive offers in our rewards section. Premium users earn points at 2x the regular rate and get access to special rewards.
