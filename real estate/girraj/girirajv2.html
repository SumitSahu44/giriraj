Project Overview

Giriraj Heights is a premium residential project offering luxurious 3 & 4 BHK apartments in the heart of Bandra West, Mumbai. Spread across 5.2 acres of prime land, this architectural masterpiece combines elegant design with modern amenities to provide an unparalleled living experience.

Each residence is meticulously crafted with high-quality materials and features spacious layouts, premium fittings, and abundant natural light. The project boasts of world-class amenities including a state-of-the-art fitness center, swimming pool, landscaped gardens, children's play area, and 24/7 security.

With its strategic location, Giriraj Heights offers excellent connectivity to major business districts, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, entertainment hubs, and transportation networks, making it an ideal choice for discerning homebuyers seeking a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience.
